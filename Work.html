Work

Here you will gain some impressions in which fields of work I am interested.

System Automation at Campusjäger GmbH

I had my first student job at Campusjäger GmbH in Karlsruhe, Germany. At the time, I started to work there, the startup was just two years old. As I went there, I was in semester two of my studies and had nearly no idea about computer science and automation. Despite my lack of knowledge, I started to work as “kind of developer” by creating and being responsible for a tool which should autonomously and intelligent spread, well formatted informations on several forums and websites.

This task was far beyond my horizon so I had to learn all about Web Automation and Selenium IDE before I could start.


Eight months later, in March 2017, that automation system was a central element of the company and responsible for a third of the inflow of total costumers. During this time, my tasks and duties also developed. From building a spreading tool, to be responsible for analysing the company KPI's, to be advice the founders in terms of marketing strategies.

I very enjoyed my time at Campusjäger GmbH. I could develop skills in:

  • Project Management
  • Business Development and Analytics
  • Web Automation and Online Marketing

Tutor at Karlsruher Institut for Technologie (KIT)

Exploring the field of an Online Developer/Business Analyst, I decited to do something very different in activity but staying in the field of Mathematics and Computer Science. My time as awardee of Stiftung Wissen+Kompetenzen showed me the importance of gaining diverse soft skills. To practice what I have learned, I became:

  • Tutor for “Operation Research I”, from Apr.2017 to Aug.2017,
  • Tutor for “Basics in Informatics II”, from Okt.2017 to Feb.2018.

As Tutor I had to explain and repeat the theorie, tought at the lecture , to a classes of 20 – 40 students. Working as a teacher made so much fun – recognizing that the preparation for the class was helpful for them is a beautiful feeling. So I invested a lot of time in processing exercises to make them easy to understand for my fellow students.

Especially soft skills and further knowledge of matter like:

  • Presentation-/Moderation Skills
  • Deep Knowlegde of subject matter
  • Skills to explain complex structurs in an easy way

were very helpful to do a good job. When reaching a evaluation score of 98 of 100 points, I felt confirmed in my choice of work
